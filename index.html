Marine Forecast Consensus Dashboard

Compare three marine model datasets, add wind in mph when available, and average the matching hourly wave data. Runs in your browser, including on your phone.

Quick spots:
Rating logic: Under 2 ft is usually Good or Fair. It only gets Caution under 2 ft when the average period is under 5 seconds, sustained wind is around 15+ mph, gusts are around 22+ mph, or model disagreement is unusually bad.
